Grading

This I’ll be looking for while I’m grading.

  1. Is your project folder organized properly? Are the necessary folders and files all there (hint: where will you st_write your files)?

  2. What are the comments like, both in r chunks and in text? Are they sufficient? Is the formatting of the markdown proper? Are the headers done properly? Are r chunks named appropriately (hint: is there a setup r chunk)?

  3. Are the maps of the three variables correct in the output of the Rmd?

  4. Are the polished maps actually polished? Is there a single (not three) north arrow and scale bar? Is there a title and your name? What is the source of your data?
